Site Plan: Temple Journey

Author: Zelalem Eshetu

Site Name

Temple Journey — a website dedicated to exploring and sharing information about LDS temples around the world.

Optional domain: templejourney.org

Site Purpose

This website helps visitors learn about different LDS temples, their history, locations, and spiritual significance. It also provides inspirational stories and tips for planning temple visits.

Scenarios

  • Which temples are closest to my current location?
  • What are the visiting hours for the Hamilton New Zealand Temple?

Color Schema

Selected colors:

  • #002B5B – Used for headings and the main background.
  • #E8F0F2 – Used for text background and accents.

Typography

  • Playfair Display – for headings (gives a classic temple feel)
  • Open Sans – for body text (simple and readable)
