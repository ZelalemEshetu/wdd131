Culture

Ethiopia is one of the oldest nations in the world with a rich history, traditions, and diverse cultural heritage. It is known for its music, dance, coffee ceremonies, and being home to more than 80 ethnic groups.

Food

Ethiopian cuisine is famous for dishes like injera (flat sourdough bread), doro wat (spicy chicken stew), and a variety of lentil and vegetable dishes. Meals are often shared from a large plate, emphasizing community and togetherness.
